HEALTH, SAFETY AND THE ENVIRONMENT

Sohar Steel Group is highly commited to safeguarding the health and safety of its employees as well as recognizing the importance of complying with the government's environmental regulations.

Through the years the group has improved its HSE processes and controls. To date it has established the following general health policy statement;

  • To Provide adequate control of the health and safety risks arising from our work activities;
  • To consult with our employees on matters affecting their health and safety;
  • To provide and maintain safe plant and equipment;
  • To ensure safe handling and use of substances;
  • To provideinformation, instruction and supervision for employees;
  • To ensure all employees are competent to do their tasks, and to give them adequate training;
  • To prevent acciddent and cases of work-related ill health




ENVIRONMENT POLICY

As an environmentally conscious organization, Sohar Steel Group shall:

  • Comply with applicable environmental laws and regulations
  • Set internal standards and requirements, when necessary
  • Apply practices and use technologies that mimimize population
  • Take corrective action wherever past practices have harmed the environment
  • Ensure that prompt action is taken whenever one of its activities endangers the health and safety of its employees, customers or the general public or the environment.
  • Make continuos improvment in its activities to prevent polluton of air, land and water
