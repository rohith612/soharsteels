SOHAR STEEL LLC


Sohar Steel LLC is located in Sohar Industrial Port Companies (SIPC) area which is adjacent to the sea port of Sohar. The plant is capable of producing 600,000 MT of Steel Billets and 500,000MT of Rebars annually. The plant also has a Cut & Bend services facility. The present plant facilities include Ultra High Power (UHP) Electric Arc Furnace (EAF) with 80 MT capacity, powered by 30 MVA transformer and Ladle Furnace (LF) of 5 MVA power. The 6 strand Caster of 6 meter radius can cast billet sizes of 100, 120 and 130 mm. Hydraulic shear is used for the automatic cutting of billets. The plant is capable of receiving scrap, DRI and HBI as raw material. The integrated Rolling Mill uses Bendotti walking beam furnace of 110MT per hour capacity.

Sohar Steel is equipped with modernized Wet Lab facilities and most modern type Spectrometer through which strict quality control measures are practiced to achieve the specified product quality standards.

Sohar Steel is accredited with ISO 9001, ISO 14001 and OHSAS 18001 certifications for the entire unit including testing facilities.
