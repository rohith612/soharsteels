COMPANY PROFILE


Middle East Conversion Industries LLC (MECI), is another unit of SSSRM consisting of an Epoxy Coating plant and a Weld Mesh plant. The plant is supplied by travelbeton SA of Italy, manufactured by Carlo Banfi SPA, Elind SPA and ZEUS Italy. The capacity of the plant is about 2500 tons.

The Fusion bonded Epoxy coating plant cater to the needs of construction industry primarily in the Sultanate of Oman. Epoxy coated rebars are used by several construction companies to prevent the rusting and corrosion of rebars in specific conditions.

MECI also produces welded steel mesh which is also a product preferred by many large scale construction companies.
